Our Services

What We Do?

Our services include but are not limited to tax planning, auditing, accounting, financial consulting, and compliance. We have a proven track record of delivering exceptional results to our clients, and we strive to maintain the highest standards of professionalism and integrity in everything we do.

Assisting Corporates (MSME) in preparing Base Resolution Plan under Pre-Pack Scheme

Our team specializes in assisting MSMEs in preparing a comprehensive Base Resolution Plan under the Pre-Pack Scheme, ensuring a streamlined and simplified process to address financial distress through debt reduction, improved cash flow, and streamlined operations.

Assessing the viability of the businesses of the Corporate Debtor under CIRP

Our team has extensive experience in assessing the viability of businesses undergoing Corporate Insolvency Resolution Process (CIRP), analyzing financial statements and operational data to provide a comprehensive report on the potential for successful restructuring or liquidation.

Drafting of Resolution Plans / Settlement Plans/ Repayment /Restructuring Plans

We specialize in drafting Resolution Plans, Settlement Plans, Repayment Plans, and Restructuring Plans for distressed companies, leveraging our expertise and experience to provide effective solutions for debt resolution and business restructuring.
